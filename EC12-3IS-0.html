Systems and their Applications

This section explores the fundamental types of intelligent systems used in enterprises, including Decision Support Systems (DSS) and Expert Systems. You'll learn about their applications, features, and how they have evolved to meet changing business needs.

Data and Intelligent Systems

Intelligent systems are powered by data. This section covers how data is transformed into usable information and knowledge within these systems, the role of a knowledge base, and the critical ethical considerations that arise from using data in this way.

Creating Intelligent Systems

This section focuses on the practical application of your knowledge. It covers the skills needed to use software to build basic Decision Support Systems and Expert Systems to solve problems for given scenarios, a key skill for your HSC examination.
