Why does it matter?

The Problem: Data Swamps

Without quality control, a "Data Lake" becomes a Data Swamp. It contains raw, unorganised, and undocumented data that is impossible to navigate or trust.

The Risk: Bias

Sampling Bias occurs when data doesn't represent the whole population. Algorithmic Bias happens when systems learn these prejudices, leading to unfair automated decisions.
